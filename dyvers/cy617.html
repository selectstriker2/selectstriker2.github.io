Greyhawk Reborn - Dyvers
GHR-DYV16-01 Lion Rampant of Westguard
by Mason Mines, Maps by Robert Kelsey
A Greyhawk Reborn 1-Round D&D Adventure set in Westguard, and Part 1 of the Westguard Ascendant story arc.
Westguard, the Westernmost keep of Dyvers and its environs has been lost to the lawless wilds of the Gnarley wood for 15 years. The overgrown cobblestones of the forest road are now tread by Undead victims of a strange plague that infects some while leaving others untouched. A one round story arc wilderness adventure set in Dyvers for Tier 1 APL 2.
