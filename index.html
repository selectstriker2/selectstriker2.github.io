GHR-DYV16-01 Lion Rampant of Westguard
by Mason Mines, Maps by Robert Kelsey
A Greyhawk Reborn 1-Round D&D Adventure set in Westguard, and Part 1 of the Westguard Ascendant story arc.
Westguard, the Westernmost keep of Dyvers and its environs has been lost to the lawless wilds of the Gnarley wood for 15 years. The overgrown cobblestones of the forest road are now tread by Undead victims of a strange plague that infects some while leaving others untouched. A one round story arc wilderness adventure set in Dyvers for Tier 1 APL 2.
GHR-DYV16-02 Inn and Outed
by Robert Kelsey
A Greyhawk Reborn 1-Round D&D Adventure set in the City of Maraven.
In Maraven they say, everything is getting better. The economy is booming, the land is fertile and rich, and trade is good, being the Gateway to the Nyr Dyv. But not everything is as it seems. Something evil is afoot, that has the citizens of the Stackery, the poor quarter of the city; making only the most needful of errands and bolting their doors after dark. A one-round adventure for Greyhawk Reborn CY 616 for level 1.
GHR-DYV16-03 Throwing Stones
by Robert Kelsey
A Greyhawk Reborn 1-Round D&D Adventure set in Dyvers and Surroundings
Deep in the Gnarley, in what used to be the lands of Dyvers, lies a former Arcane Academy in what is now a turgid swamp. No one knows for sure what haunts the Gnarley these days, but that is where our intrepid heroes must go. A one round dungeon adventure set in the Lands of Dyvers during CY 616 for levels 1 & 2.
GHR-DYV-16-04 Return to Exile
by Mason Mines
A Greyhawk Reborn One-Round D&D Adventure set in Leardyn Manor of Dyvers, and Part 2 of the Westlands Rising story arc.
The last time the lighthouse on Merroc's rock was opened was 22 years ago. The adventurers who went in returned with tribal tattoos and stories of a crystal menageri of ferral halflings and otherworldly creatures. The door to the lighthouse has been opened once more. A Tactical adventure during CY 616 for levels 1-4.
GHR-DYV16-05 High Stakes
by Derrek Burrows
edited by Mason Mines and Jay Stypinski
A Greyhawk Reborn 2-Round D&D Adventure set in Dyvers, and Part 1 of the Sword of Chaos story arc.
A Dyvers spy has reported that the Blade of Chaos, stolen years ago from the Dyvers temple of Norebo, has returned to the City. Anyone who retrieves this lost artifact of chaos can expect great fortune from the God of Gambles. The criminal bosses of Dyvers’ gritty underworld are stacking the deck against you. An Urban adventure set during CY 615 for levels 1-4.
GHR-DYV16-06 Wild Westlands
by Derreck Burrows with Jay Stypinski
A Greyhawk Reborn Stand-Alone One-Round D&D Adventure set in West of Dyvers.
Caltaran’s Sheriff Krembo Schift has a reputation of playing judge, jury and executioner for criminals on the Low Road. Lord Enruhl Leardyn has had enough, and sends some heroes to intervene. Can the adventurers save the bandits from the sheriff’s deadly deputies? A Stand-Alone/Universal/Wilderness adventure set West of Dyvers during CY 616 for levels 3-10.
GHR-DYV16-07 Easy Road

by Alan Costin and Jay Stypinski with Ken Young

A Greyhawk Reborn 1-Round D&D Adventure set in Dyvers, and Part 1 of the Southguard story arc. Southguard, southern most keep of Dyvers, abandoned 15 years ago during the Pomarj invasion. The Gnarley Forest is now overrun by Turrosh Maks’ abandoned army. A one round story arc wilderness adventure set in Dyvers for Tier 1 APL 2-4.
